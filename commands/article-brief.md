# /article-brief

Convert research findings into a publishable article brief.

## Inputs

- Findings grid
- Target audience
- Publishing format

## Steps

1. Pick the hook.
2. State the thesis.
3. Select strongest evidence.
4. Explain the mechanism.
5. Add caveats.
6. End with next-move prediction.

## Output

- Title options
- One-sentence thesis
- Outline
- Draft intro
- Source list
