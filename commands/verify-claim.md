# /verify-claim

Check one high-stakes claim across source categories.

## Inputs

- Claim
- Why it matters
- Minimum confidence needed

## Steps

1. Identify exact claim.
2. Find primary source.
3. Find corroborating or contradicting source.
4. Check definitions and dates.
5. Assign confidence.

## Output

- Verdict
- Sources
- Confidence
- Caveats
- What would resolve uncertainty
